About "Final Frontier"

This page dives into my personal exploration into the world of visual coding. Using a sound reactive model, you can expect objects to bounce and dance around with the sound or music you feed it.

The outcome has developed into a project that i would like to call the Final Frontier. It utilizes 3D shapes with shader distortions that reacts to frequency in the most colourful of ways.

This is accompanied by the various scenes which can be called through your number keys '1' through '4'.

Have a look at my process log, my p5js sketches, and github repo.
